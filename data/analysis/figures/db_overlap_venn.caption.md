**Figure 1. Five gold-standard surface-protein databases agree on only 188 proteins genome-wide.** Five-way ellipse Venn over each database's native (pre-recalibration) binary surface flag. Each set is the UniProt accessions a source labels plasma-membrane surface under its source-specific rule (Methods): UniProt, strict surface terms or extracellular topology; GO CC, non-IEA annotation to a surface-relevant cellular-component term; HPA, Enhanced/Supported/Approved reliability for plasma membrane or cell junctions; SURFY, published binary-classifier output; CSPA, high-confidence or putative. The SurfaceBench-tuned recalibration applied in later figures is not used here.
