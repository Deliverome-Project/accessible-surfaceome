direct_multi_method
grade rationale
CD81 surface accessibility is supported by multiple independent direct methods from multiple independent sources. (1) Live-cell flow cytometry (non-permeabilized) on HepG2, HEK293T, AML primary bone marrow blasts, and retinal organoid precursors across many studies confirms plasma membrane surface expression. (2) Surface biotinylation (membrane-impermeable Sulfo-NHS-SS-biotin) with streptavidin pulldown and LC-MS/MS or WB confirms surface-accessible CD81 in fibroblasts, glioma lines, and PBMC-adjacent protocols. (3) Immunoprecipitation from intact cells (original 1990 discovery) and immunogold EM on EVs provide additional orthogonal surface-detection evidence. No contradictory intracellular-only or conflicting evidence is present. The convergence of live-cell flow cytometry and surface biotinylation/MS from independent laboratories across diverse cell types and primary human samples firmly establishes direct_multi_method grade.
