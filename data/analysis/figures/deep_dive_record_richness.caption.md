**Each deep-dive record is information-dense, and surface-yes calls draw on a deeper evidence base than non-surface ones.** This MOCK figure summarises a gene's record along five richness axes: papers found in discovery, papers selected for full-text reading, selected papers with extracellular evidence, structured catalogue filters holding a value, and precomputed structural features populated. Panels (a) and (b) contrast non-surface versus surface-positive records; panels (c)–(e) show the surface-positive subset only. Dark dots are real values from 20 published deep dives; each violin is a synthetic full-cohort sketch. Only violins are synthetic in panels (b)–(e); panel (a) is fully synthetic, as discovery-search size was never stored and is drawn from the reported methods-section median of 234.5 (range roughly 50 to 400).
