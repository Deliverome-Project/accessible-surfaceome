**The public surfaceome viewer at [surfaceome.deliverome.org](https://surfaceome.deliverome.org) puts the catalog and its per-gene evidence in front of any reader, with the full open-source Next.js site shipped in the project repository.** (a) The homepage is a sortable, filterable catalog of every candidate-universe gene, showing for each one the per-source surface flags from the five gating databases (UniProt, GO Cellular Component, SURFY, CSPA, and the Human Protein Atlas), its database vote count, the triage agent's verdict with a short reason, and whether a deep-dive page exists; a "More filters" rail layers on the structured fields of the deep-dive records — roughly two dozen agent-graded filters (surface verdict, confidence, surface-call reason, expression level and breadth, secreted or shed form, epitope masking, tumor association, and more) alongside the tool-derived attributes (extracellular-domain accessibility class, cross-species and paralog domain-identity bands, binding-site targetability, homomer state) — with each control labelled by whether it is agent-graded or tool-derived. (b) The per-gene detail page expands one record into its agent-written executive summary and synthesis sections plus a precomputed-features card carrying seven tool-derived structural blocks: canonical and per-isoform membrane topology, AlphaFold confidence with structure links, mouse and cynomolgus orthologs, close paralogs by extracellular-domain identity, SURFACE-Bind binding-patch scores (Balbi et al. 2026, PMID 41604262), and AlphaFold2 homo-oligomer state (Schweke et al. 2024, PMID 38325366).
