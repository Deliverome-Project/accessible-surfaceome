**Triage-yes genes overwhelmingly confirm as surface once the deep dive reads full text, the two stages' reason calls agree about half the time, and every source — Sonnet triage and all five databases — agrees with the deep dive more strongly on its higher-confidence tiers.** The layout stacks two compact panels on the left (a over c) beside the large reason confusion matrix (b), which spans the full height on the right. Panel (a) is a verdict-flow view: one slim 100%-stacked bar per triage verdict (yes, contextual, no), split by the deep-dive's five-tier call (canonical, likely, low, uncertain, no). Triage-yes resolves to about 64% canonical plus 25% likely; triage-contextual spreads across the likely / low / no middle tiers; triage-no stays mostly no but a minority are upgraded to likely or low. Panel (b) is a confusion matrix of the triage reason (rows) against the deep-dive's re-derived reason (columns), both drawn from one shared vocabulary; reasons are grouped into surface-yes, context-dependent, and surface-no blocks so a reason swap that keeps the verdict is distinguishable from a changed verdict, and the maroon diagonal marks exact agreement (about 50% of genes). Panel (c) breaks database concordance down by deep-dive tier: for each source — the five catalog databases (UniProt, GO, SURFY, CSPA, HPA) plus Sonnet triage — a group of three tier-colored bars gives the percentage of the deep dive's canonical (n=372), likely (n=290), and low (n=221) genes that the source also flags surface. The point is whether agreement is higher on the deep dive's higher-confidence tiers, and it is, monotonically, for all six sources: every source flags a larger share of canonical genes than likely genes, and more likely than low. Sonnet triage stays the most concordant partner across the board (about 99% of canonical, 93% of likely, 86% of low), and among the databases UniProt is closest (about 99% / 75% / 50%); the gap between a source's canonical bar and its low bar is a direct read on how tightly that source tracks the deep dive's own confidence. The triage verdict and reason come from the genome-wide Sonnet triage run in the public database; the deep-dive reason and tier come from the deep-dive records, and the five per-DB surface flags come from the candidate-universe catalog.
