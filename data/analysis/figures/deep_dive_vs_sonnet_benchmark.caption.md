(a) Overall accuracy: the deep dive is correct on 96% of the 27 genes versus 93% for Sonnet+NCBI on the same genes. (b) Accuracy split by ground-truth bucket: both are perfect on the clear-surface (yes) genes (n=17); the deep dive's edge is on the context-dependent (contextual) genes (100% versus 88%, n=8), where reading the full text resolves the state-dependence the triage can only guess at; both miss one of the two not-surface (no) genes. The deep-dive verdict counts as surface when its tier is canonical, likely, or low (otherwise not-surface); Sonnet and the ground truth count as surface when yes or contextual.
