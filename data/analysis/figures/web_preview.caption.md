**The public surfaceome viewer at [surfaceome.deliverome.org](https://surfaceome.deliverome.org) puts the catalog and its per-gene evidence in front of any reader.** (a) The homepage is a sortable, filterable catalog of every candidate-universe gene, showing per-source flags from the five gating databases (UniProt, GO Cellular Component, SURFY, CSPA, Human Protein Atlas), its database vote count, the triage agent's verdict and reason, whether a deep-dive page exists, and a "More filters" rail of agent-graded and tool-derived fields. (b) The per-gene page expands one record into its agent-written executive summary and synthesis plus a card of seven tool-derived structural blocks: canonical and per-isoform membrane topology, AlphaFold confidence, mouse and cynomolgus orthologs, close paralogs by extracellular-domain identity, SURFACE-Bind binding-patch scores (Balbi et al. 2026, PMID 41604262), and AlphaFold2 homo-oligomer state (Schweke et al. 2024, PMID 38325366).
